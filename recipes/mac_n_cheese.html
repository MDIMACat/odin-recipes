Mac and Cheese

Description

A easy mac and cheese recipe that will have the whole family asking for seconds. Creamy, comforting and delicious, this slow cooker recipe is great for family gatherings or a cozy night in.

Simple to make and great tasting, why wouldnt you try it

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package elbow macaroni
  • Â½ cup butter
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 (16 ounce) package shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
  • 1(5 ounce) can evaporated milk
  • 2 eggs, well beaten
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed Cheddar cheese soup (such as Campbell's)
  • 1 pinch paprika, or as desired (Optional)

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil
  2. Stir in macaroni and return to a boil.
  3. Cook pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes
  4. Drain and transfer pasta to a slow cooker.
In slow cooker
  1. Add butter to pasta and stir until melted; season with salt and pepper
  2. Sprinkle about 1/2 of the Cheddar cheese over pasta and stir.
In a bowls
  1. Whisk evaporated milk and eggs together in a bowl until smooth
  2. stir into pasta mixture.
  3. Whisk milk and condensed soup together in a bowl until smooth
  4. stir into pasta mixture.
In slow cooker
  1. Sprinkle remaining cheese over pasta mixture; garnish with paprika.
  2. Cook on Low for 3 hours.

Serve hot and enjoy
