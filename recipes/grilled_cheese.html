Jalapeño Popper Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

This is for all the spicy food lovers out there. If you enjoy a good sandwich and a huge cheese lover, than this recipe is perfect for YOU

A spicy twist to our more traditional Grilled Cheese Sandwich, the Jalapeño pepper influenced sandwich wont only satify your cravings for spicy food, but the goowy cheese will satify any cheese lover

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1 tablespoon sour cream
  • 10 pickled jalapeno pepper slices, or to taste - chopped
  • 2 ciabatta sandwich rolls
  • 4 teaspoons butter
  • 8 tortilla chips, crushed
  • ½ cup shredded Colby-Monterey Jack cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat a skillet over medium heat.
In a bowl
  1. Combine cream cheese, sour cream, and pickled jalapeno in a small bowl.
  2. Set aside.
On a Cutting Board
  1. Slice each roll in half horizontally, then slice the rounded tops off the ciabatta rolls to make a flat top half.
  2. Spread 1 teaspoon butter on the doughy cut side of the bottom bun and 1 teaspoon butter on the now flattened top bun.
  3. Place 1/2 of the cream cheese mixture, 1/2 of the crushed chips, and 1/2 of the shredded cheese on the non-buttered side of the bottom bun.
  4. Place the top half of the bun on the sandwich and place the sandwich on the hot skillet.
  5. Repeat with the second sandwich.
In skillet
  1. Grill until lightly browned and flip over, about 3 to 5 minutes or continue grilling until cheese is melted and the second side is golden brown.

Serve hot and enjoy
